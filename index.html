About Avalanche Academy

The Avalanche Academy consists of 10,000 randomly generated LAB APES living on the Avalanche network. LAB APES can be found with a variety of different colours, outfits, and faces but no two LAB APES are alike. Each LAB Ape brings their own style to the Avalanche Academy with unique hats, hair, clothing, and faces from a total of 144 different attributes!

TOKENOMICS

Avalanche Academy uses an all new reflectionary minting system that earns ape holders LAB !
This means that 10% of every minting fee is reflected back to existing LAB Apes holders and can be claimed at any time!
The rewards don't stop when minting is complete! In our marketplace original minters of LAB APES earn 1% royalties each time their LAB Ape is resold and 3% of each sale is redistributed between all LAB Ape holders.

PARITY RANKING

At the Avalanche Academy we don't have any dress codes, every ape is welcome! That said, some LAB APES have more style than others.

LAB APES are scored on a scale of 3 to 13 points. The ranking and background color of your LAB Ape is determined by the total score of all attribute scores. Rarer items or features like an astronaut suit will score higher than common items like a bandana.
